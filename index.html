This is not about you.

But before the great company named NOWCOM I was a Homebase employee of an AU web dev company its a secret so I will just give the first letter, letter B. In that company, I also do WordPress but more on Divi builder plugin, but there's a moment that they assign me to create a plugin to extend the woo-commerce plugin, then before that B company, I was a regular web developer of IConcept Global Industries, Inc. I stay for that company for 5 years, I learn so much with them in terms of WordPress theme development, because we develop a WordPress site from scratch we don't use any builder plugin. Also, I gain lots of friends there and happy memories that will keep poreber(bit emotional in this part).
